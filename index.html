The Intersection of Code and Math

We don't just solve equations. We build algorithms, train models, and engineer systems based on absolute logic.

Ekasutram is the official technical and mathematics community of VIT Pune. We believe that behind every great piece of software, efficient algorithm, or AI model, there is elegant mathematics. We host hackathons, coding bootcamps, algothons, and deep-dive seminars into everything from neural networks to competitive programming.

Technical Excellence

To cultivate elite coders, machine learning researchers, and mathematical thinkers who can solve complex engineering challenges through structured logic.

Open Innovation

Organizing 24-hour hackathons, competitive programming contests (CP), and building open-source tools that empower our student developer community.

AI & Algorithms

Democratizing knowledge in Artificial Intelligence by bridging the gap between pure mathematics (linear algebra, calculus) and machine learning deployment.
